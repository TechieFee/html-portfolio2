Best Movies According To Felicia

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Sudden Fear

This is my favorite movie because Joan Crawford is my favorite actress of the early 1900s. She plays a rich woman who with time warms up to a man and decides to marry him until she finds out that he is planning to kill her for her fortune. She then flips the script and plots on her revenge. A definite must see film.

Dirty Dancing

The main character, Baby, lives a sheltered life of a teenage girl from a wealthy family. Her family goes on a cabin retreat every year with other elite members, which is usually very boring to her. This time she is surprised to meet a young man that will change her life played by Patrick Swayze.

Not Another Teen Movie

This is a hilarious movie that mimics all of the teen drama movies from the 90s. It uses scenes from the movies and turns it into comedy. This is a must see if you love to laugh.
